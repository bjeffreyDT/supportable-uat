STORY 8. CONTACT LOGS AND NOTES

OBJECTIVE

Create contact logs and notes.


INSTRUCTIONS

This is where contact logs and internal notes are entered and created.
  1. Navigate to Supportable with any web browser. Google Chrome is always the most optimal option. Here is the link.
  2. Use your credentials to enter your username and password.
  3. Read the ISSUES/DESIGN FLAWS WE'RE ALREADY AWARE OF section below.
  4. Open a referral that has made it past the "New" stage.
  5. Click the View Referral Details button.
  6. Click the Contacts tab.
  7. Without more intruction, process any contacts. Make a few up. Is this intuitive enough to figure out yourself? Can you figure out how to designate an emergency contact?
  8. When you feel you've gathered enough impressions, fill out and submit the UAT Feedback Form first by choosing Story 8. Contact Logs and Notes in the first dropdown.



ISSUES/DESIGN NOTES WE'RE ALREADY AWARE OF

  1. When opening a referral in the screening stage, the View Referral Details button defaults to highlighted. This will be fixed. The button will also be anchored to the bottom of the screen so you won't have to scroll to get to it.
  2. Links are not on-brand. They are currently blue but will match style of the rest of the application.
  3. The hover effect on buttons is not finalized but will be consistent across the application.
  4. The date and time format will be mm/dd/yyyy 12:00am
  5. Final consistent styling of lists are in development, this means all lists will have the same look and feel. Currently, the headers on this list do not stand out from the list item content.
